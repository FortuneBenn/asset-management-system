Welcome to the University of Mpumalanga Assets Management System (UMAMS)

About UMAMS

The University of Mpumalanga Assets Management System (UMAMS) is designed to streamline the management of university assets. This platform empowers administrators, staff, and technicians to efficiently track, monitor, and manage assets to ensure optimal utilization and maintenance.

Features

  • Centralized asset management for improved visibility.
  • Role-based access for administrators, staff, and technicians.
  • Comprehensive repair and maintenance tracking.
  • Automated notifications for asset-related updates.

Our Mission

UMAMS aims to enhance operational efficiency at the University of Mpumalanga by providing a robust and user-friendly platform for managing university assets. By utilizing this system, we hope to:

  • Reduce asset downtime and improve maintenance efficiency.
  • Enhance accountability and transparency in asset usage.
  • Provide actionable insights for better decision-making.

Get Started

Log in to explore the features of UMAMS tailored to your role:

  • Administrators: Oversee asset management and generate reports.
  • Staff: View assigned assets and report issues.
  • Technicians: Manage repair requests and updates.
